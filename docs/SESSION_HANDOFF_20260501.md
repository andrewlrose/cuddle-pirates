## 4. The Three Merger Proposals — Summary

All three preserve the Pirates as fully realized autonomous beings.

**Proposal A — "The Daytime Play Sets the Stage"**
Each episode opens in daytime imagination mode (= Laundry Day format, exactly).
At night, the real Ann Bunny arrives and is nothing like what Junie imagined.
The stuffed Ann Bunny slowly acquires the real one's specific knowledge across
the season — without Junie noticing. Two visual modes per episode (flat/bright
daytime; cool/atmospheric night). Theme: *Your imagination is good. The real
thing is better.*

**Proposal B — "The House Is Already an Ocean"**
Original premise intact; territory expands. The hypnagogic state extends through
the whole house on nights Junie is deeply between worlds. Each room has a crew
name (the Narrows, the Freshwater Reaches, the Forbidden Latitude, the Galley
of Giants). The backyard is uncharted — Ava's territory is larger than expected.
Simplest integration path. No premise change required.

**Proposal C — "The Parallel Tracks"**
Night episodes (original format) and daytime episodes (Laundry Day format)
coexist. The daytime play accidentally solves real problems the Pirates set up
the night before — Junie is working from memory she doesn't know she has.
Ann Bunny is leaving breadcrumbs. Drake has noticed. The coincidences are the
series arc. Most ambitious of the three; richest long-term.
