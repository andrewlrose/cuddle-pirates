Cuddle Pirate Theater
The Cuddle Pirates are a merry band of plushie pirates that commandeer bedtime
and put on a show to tell their bedtime stories, and then when everyone is asleep,
they steal all the comfiest pillows and softest blankies in all the world, to take
their fluffy plunder back to Puff-tooga, their Cuddle Pirate Island.

One night, they swoop in on little Juniper. While dodging Mom so they remain
invisible, they entertain her with an acrobatic ragdoll antics show. With Mom
distracted across the hall doing laundry. Juniper admits to the Cuddles that she
is sad about her missing sock, and the Cuddle Pirates tell her a bedtime story.
